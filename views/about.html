WELCOME!

Philosophy

Baking with passion! That is how we start the business. We make our product with love and an eye for detail - baked to perfection. We treated our freshly baked breads, cookies, and cakes meticulously on the premise with top quality ingredients. We use only the freshest, finest, and ripest ingredients to ensure you have exceptional flavor on every bite.

History

In 2007, Doctype Deserts Bakery opened its doors to the Vancouver market at 1234 Bakery Lane by Ryan and Eric. Ryan is a third generation baker, and his grandfather operated a bakery in Vancouver since 1925. Ryan and Eric, Ryan's cousin, opened their bakery in 2007. Eric worked as a technical consultant for a bakery ingredient company before opening Doctype Deserts Bakery.Neda and Elizabeth have joined them since 2008.

Chef

Neda grew up in Iran, around a family that loved to cook, and she was introduced to the food industry at the age of 18. She run a small bakery in Iran, but she moved to Canada in 2005. Finally, she joined the successful Doctype Deserts bakery in 2008.
In 2000, Elizabeth completed her baking apprenticeship at Accent on Pastries in downtown Langley and went on to work in a number of Vancouver restaurants and bakeries before joining this team.
