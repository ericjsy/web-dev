WELCOME!

Philosophy

Baking with passion! That is how we started the business. We make our products with love and an eye for detail - baked to perfection. We treat our freshly baked breads, cookies, and cakes meticulously with top quality ingredients: the freshest, finest, and ripest ingredients to ensure you have exceptional flavor on every bite.

History

In 2007, Ryan and Eric unveiled !DOCTYPE Desserts by opening its doors to the Vancouver market at 1234 Bakery Lane. Ryan is a third generation baker, and his grandfather operated a bakery in Vancouver since 1925. Eric worked as a technical consultant for a bakery ingredient company before opening !DOCTYPE Desserts Bakery. Neda and Elizabeth joined them since 2008 to create the core four members of the the team. Together, Neda and Elizabeth- two world-famous bakers- bring a combined 20+ years of baking experience!

Chef

Neda grew up in Iran, around a family that loved to cook, and she was introduced to the food industry at the age of 18. She ran a small bakery in Iran, but she moved to Canada in 2005. Finally, she joined the successful !DOCTYPE Desserts in 2008.

In 2000, Elizabeth completed her baking apprenticeship at Accent on Pastries in downtown Langley and went on to work in a number of Vancouver restaurants and bakeries before joining this team.
